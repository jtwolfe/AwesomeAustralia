# Acceleration Priorities

Front-load high-leverage actions so compounding gains arrive sooner.

## Highest Near-Term ROI

1. **Energy abundance** — master resource. Nuclear (advanced/SMR), reliable baseload, whatever works at system scale.
2. **Remove artificial housing scarcity** — zoning, planning reform, by-right mid-density, concurrent infrastructure. Highest immediate impact on living standards, fertility, and political pressure.
3. **Maximise AI/robotics deployment** with institutions that keep humans high-agency rather than clients.
4. **Capture resource rents for citizens** — genuine SWF + potential dividends so geological luck compounds into broad surplus.
5. **Selection and cultural transmission** favouring competence, low time-preference, reciprocity, and future-orientation.
6. **Minimal high-capacity core state** that protects the conditions for abundance without expanding into voluntary layers.

## Sequencing Logic

Abundance in energy and housing reduces scarcity psychology and the demand for coercive redistribution. High selection and trust make low-coercion systems viable. Productivity and automation free human time for higher Maslow levels and frontier expansion. Cultural substrate determines whether material abundance expands agency or produces stagnation/dependency.

Strive for acceleration while remaining reality-constrained. Physics, incentives, and human nature still apply.
