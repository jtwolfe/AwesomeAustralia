# Progress Ladder: Maslow + Timelines Toward Abundance

Inspired by post-scarcity frames such as Iain M. Banks’ Culture series, while remaining constrained by physics, energy, coordination, and human nature.

## Deleting Scarcity via Maslow

1. **Physiological** (food, water, shelter, energy, basic health) — most deletable via technology + institutions.
2. **Safety** (physical security, stable order) — core state functions + cultural norms.
3. **Belonging** — selection, cultural transmission, voluntary association.
4. **Esteem** — competence, status, recognition (partly relative; abundance changes the game but does not eliminate hierarchy).
5. **Self-actualisation** — time, surplus, and high-agency culture.

## Timeline Ladder (Realistic-to-Speculative)

**Months–2 years**  
Regulatory clearance for housing elasticity and key energy projects. Deployment of existing AI into knowledge work/logistics. Targeted robotics in agriculture/mining. Begin resource-rent reforms. Visible falls in real housing costs and energy constraints.

**3–10 years**  
General-purpose robotics and advanced AI agents displace large categories of routine labour. Energy systems shift toward higher density/reliability. Food production far less labour-intensive. Sharp per-capita gains possible if institutions do not block deployment.

**10–30 years**  
Most material goods and many services approach near-zero marginal cost in advanced settings. Human labour optional for survival/basic comfort. Political/cultural problem becomes distribution of remaining scarce goods (status, unique experiences, desirable land) and prevention of new control forms. Space resources become economically relevant.

**30–100+ years**  
If alignment and coordination succeed: Culture-like abundance (energy/materials at planetary/stellar scale, AI managing complexity). Biological enhancement, radical life extension, multi-planetary presence. Risks shift to meaning, selection, value drift.

**Centuries–millennia**  
Speculative full post-scarcity material economy, possible expansion beyond the solar system. Physics still binds (light-speed, thermodynamics). Human experience can be arbitrarily rich or empty depending on embedded values.

Absolute US-scale GDP for Australia in 4–18 years is unrealistic (order-of-magnitude population and scale gap). Per-capita leadership and rapid deletion of material scarcities is the coherent target.
